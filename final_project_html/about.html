About Us

Starting over 25 years ago, Florella has earned a name synonymous with quality and style. During that time we have developed the best processes and practices in designing and manufacturing artificial flowers, faux plants and home fragrances. Perfection takes time and we have had made years in perfecting the art form of artificial creativity.

How to buy.

Florella is a wholesale business and therefore allows online store access only to businesses, organizations and retailers. Shopping online is easy, just sign up and we will review your details and approve your account allowing you access to our online store. Once access is approved you may select from our beautiful range of artificial flowers and plants, add products to your cart, review your purchases and proceed to the checkout. It's that simple.

Trade Fairs

We exhibit at several trade fairs annually showcasing our exquisite artificial flowers.
